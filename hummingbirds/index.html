Hummingbirds

Found a Hummingbird?

  • In front of a window.
    • keep an eye on it for 10 minutes. If no response try offering some sugar water (1 part white sugar mixed with 4 parts water). Offer sugar water in a bottle cap (dont let mixture get on its feathers) or in a syringe. gently dip tip of beak into water (dont hold it there) and see if it will drink. If it drinks, wait another 10 minutes to see if it will fly off. If no improvement, bring to a wildlife center
  • On the Ground
    • If it is spring time, most likely it is a fledgling learning to fly. Leave it where it is or gently scoop into hands and place in a bush nearby.
    • if caught by a cat, bring into wildlife center. even if there are no apparent wounds, any break in the skin can allow bacteria from the cats teeth to slowly kill the bird
  • under/around a tree or bush in the spring
    • leave it alone unless youve seen another animal attack it.
